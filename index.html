About

Chess is loved by players all over the world. It is the most beloved board game! Chess improves strategic thinking, problem solving skills, and also improves the part of the brain that helps visualise objects.

CheckMate uses stockfish, a powerful chess engine. It is one of the most, if not the most, popular chess engines. Since stockfish is integrated into CheckMate, players can improve fast, and turn into exceptional chess players!

CheckMate was created by Yash Patel, a 14 year-old programmer.
